## 📌 Core Research Question

How does student performance in **synchronous** and **asynchronous** online courses compare to **face-to-face** courses in the California Community Colleges (CCC) system, and how have these performance gaps changed over time (pre- vs. post-pandemic)? Additionally, how do these gaps vary across student subgroups defined by race/ethnicity and financial aid use?
